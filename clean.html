About

My name is Erik Skår.
I'm a 16 year student who is currently in High School, and in my spare time I enjoy playing videogames and doing web design / programming. This year of HS I'm taking a course in IT, which so far is really fun and exciting. We are currently working with HTML and CSS. I've got some other programming experiences as well, including Python, in which I've made a couple of small and handy programs. I've got some basic knowledge of Javascript, which is barely enough to get me a couple of steps further in web development, but for the most part I just look up snippets on SO and tweak them until they fit my project. I've tried to teach myself some C#, but I just can't seem to get into it. One of my main obstacles is that I mainly program / develop on my laptop, which is currently running Linux Mint. As you might know, C# is based on the .NET framework, which isn't natively supported on Linux. In addition to this, I just haven't been able to get into the mindset of C#, and I find Python to be way easier to just pick up and make programs with.
When it comes to other hobbies I've had quite a few over the last couple of years. I've been on the National Team in diving, which is basically all there is for athletic hobbies. However I have quite a lot of experience with image and video editing / manipulation. I've been mainly been using Photoshop and Premiere Pro, but I do also have experience with After Effects, Vegas Pro and Gimp.
Apart from all of that I enjoy spending my time just fooling around technology, especially software, and just learning new things. For my next OS I will probably try Arch Linux, however I am concerned about the fact that most of the stuff that I'm currently used to won't work.
